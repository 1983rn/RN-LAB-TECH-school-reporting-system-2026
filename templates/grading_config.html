Guidelines
  • Forms 1 & 2 use letter grades (A, B, C, D, F). 50% is the standard pass mark (Grade D).
  • Forms 3 & 4 use MSCE numerical points (1-9). Grade 1-8 are passes, Grade 9 is a fail.
  • Recalculation: Changing these rules will immediately affect all report cards generated from now on, including for marks already in the system.
